About Project

This project is the final practical work under the Girls Coding Course. We are basing it as a tribute website for the Heart of North, Kulhudhuffushi City. This website consists of a lot of information that brightens up the golden memories of Kulhudhuffushi City and its Legends. To make it more realistic, relevant pictures from day to day from past years to the present have been included. This website will not only give information about Kulhudhuffushi, but it will also help visitors to reach out and know more about this City.
